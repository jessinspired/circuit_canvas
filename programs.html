Smart Girls Tech Club

A community based technopreneurship mentorship Program for teenage girls to empower and provide them with a strong foundation in ICT upon which to build their careers as computer programmers, developers,problem solvers, creative and become innovative techpreneurs

Smart Girls Tech Club

Starting 18th May, 2025

Tech program 15-18 years SDGs
Get in touch Start a free trial

Program Description

The program provides girls with a safe environment to be mentored, support each other as they learn, as well as  provide the platform to network with millions of girls across the world that are using technology to solve problems they care about.

This program is for ..

High school girls who are

  • Innovative
  • Creative
  • Determined to TAKE ACTION
  • Ready to showcase affordable, innovative technology solutions to solve SDGs in their community.

Eligibility Criteria

Must be in High School when applying for the program.

Resources you need for this program

  • A Laptop or tablet.
  • A pair of headphones

*Exercises require a computer with internet access

Schedule

Date: Saturdays

Time: 12pm - 2pm

Location: Edinburgh

Benefits of the course

  • Participants will learn the fundamentals of Computer Science
  • Work on assignments in teams to create technology based impact projects that will address the SDG GOAL problems in their immediate communities.

Activities

  • Tech Class
  • Educational Tours
  • Demo Day 
  • Global Tournaments
