Robotics

Date: 13th Feb - 24th Mar, 2025

online 15-18 years SDGs
Get in touch Start a free trial

Course Description

Robotics and artificial intelligence have become part of our daily lives, it is now been used in the offices, homes and security agencies. This course gives comprehensive guild on robotics and the application of artificial intelligence in automating devices for smart homes, smart offices, smart farms, smart hospitals and smart factories etc., it also covers Internet of Thing (IoT), and gives indebt understanding on how electronics works and their application areas. The students will learn how to apply the knowledge in solving world problems, become innovative by creating problem solving robots that mimics human and creating automated machines (Robots).

This course is for ..

  • Everyone who wants to explore the world of Robotics and Artificial Intelligence
  • Curious minds wanting to learn Robotics
  • Robotics engineers wanting to improve their skills
  • Tech savvies
  • Those who love drones and are willing to learn how to build and fly them
  • Those interested in 3D printing
  • Everyone interested in constructing smart home devices like voice-activated lights, smart waste bins, automated light switches, obstacle detection devices, theft detection, fire alarms, to mention but a few

Eligibility Criteria

  • Basic Knowledge of computer is required for beginners

Resources you need for this program

  • Laptop (Computer)
    • Windows 7, Windows 8, Windows 10
    • 2 GB of RAM minimum (4GB recommended)
  • Robotics Kits (with full set of sensors)
  • Writing Materials

Benefits of the course

  • Understand automation
  • Use Internet of Things in solving world problems
  • Understand Drone technology and how to build and fly them
  • Understand 3D Printing Technology
  • Compete with other robotics experts both local and international
  • Create automated devices capable of replacing human efforts
  • Become innovative

Program Description

The program provides girls with a safe environment to be mentored, support each other as they learn, as well as  provide the platform to network with millions of girls across the world that are using technology to solve problems they care about.

This program is for ..

High school girls who are

  • Innovative
  • Creative
  • Determined to TAKE ACTION
  • Ready to showcase affordable, innovative technology solutions to solve SDGs in their community.

Eligibility Criteria

Must be in High School when applying for the program.

Resources you need for this program

  • A Laptop or tablet.
  • A pair of headphones

*Exercises require a computer with internet access

Schedule

Date: Saturdays

Time: 12pm - 2pm

Location: Edinburgh

Benefits of the course

  • Participants will learn the fundamentals of Computer Science
  • Work on assignments in teams to create technology based impact projects that will address the SDG GOAL problems in their immediate communities.
